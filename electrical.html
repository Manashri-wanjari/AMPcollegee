About Department

An Electrical Engineering department focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems, equipment, and devices. It encompasses a broad range of areas, from power generation and distribution to telecommunications and control systems. These departments typically offer diplomas in electrical engineering, preparing students for careers in various industries, PSUs and power sector.

🎯

Educational Objectives

  • Provide a strong foundation in the fundamentals of electrical engineering.
  • Equip students with the technical skills needed to design, develop, and analyze electrical systems.
  • Cultivate problem-solving and critical-thinking abilities.
  • Prepare students for successful careers in various industries.
👁️

Vision

To produce competent technical manpower in Electrical Engineering for fulfilling needs of industry and society

🚀

Mission

  • To impart need based technical education in Electrical Engineering.
  • To modernize departmental laboratories and facilities.
  • To inculcate values and ethics among students.
📘

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Provide socially responsible, environment friendly broad-based solutions to Computer engineering related problems adapting professional ethics.
  • Adapt to emerging automation technologies.
  • Serve in industries, utilities & service sectors.
📈

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • Analyze & maintain electrical systems.
  • Use modern testing & simulation tools.
  • Work effectively in engineering teams.
🔧

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Operate and maintain electrical machines.
  • Apply wiring, installation & safety practices.
  • Electrical Equipment:Maintain various types of rotating and static electrical equipment.
  • Electric Power Systems:Maintain different types of electrical power systems.
