About Polytechnic College

Dr. Arun Motghare Polytechnic, Kondha-Kosra was established with the objective of providing quality technical education and skill-based learning to students from rural and semi-urban areas.

The institute offers diploma-level technical programs with a strong emphasis on practical training, modern laboratories, and industry-oriented education supported by experienced faculty members.

The polytechnic is committed to developing competent technicians with professional ethics, innovation, and lifelong learning skills to meet industry and societal needs.

From Principal's Desk

Dr. A. L. Motghare

Dr. Arun Motghare, Founder of Late Shri Laxmanji Motghare Charitable Trust, Nagpur, established Dr. Arun Motghare Polytechnic in the year 2009 with the aim of providing quality technical education to students from rural and economically weaker sections. Located at Kondha-Kosra, District Bhandara, the institute is one of the pioneering technical institutions in the surrounding region.

The Polytechnic is committed to delivering skill-based education with a strong focus on practical training, discipline, and academic excellence. With experienced faculty, well-equipped laboratories, and a supportive learning environment, the institute encourages overall development of students.

Along with technical education, the institute actively participates in social welfare activities through the Trust, contributing to the betterment of society. Dr. Arun Motghare Polytechnic continues to grow steadily, nurturing skilled professionals with strong values and social responsibility.

Come, join ACP and be a part of our journey to personal as well as national progress.
