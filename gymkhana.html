About Gymkhana

Our gymkhana is an open platform for the students of the institute to exhibit their talent by participating in various events such as interclass and IEDSSA sports, cultural events (Annual Gathering), debate competition, poster competition, technical exhibition, and social events such as new voter registration and awareness campaign.

Facilities available in Gymkhana

  • Sports materials and kits for various game events
  • Musical instruments
  • Provision to send selected students to take part in inter diploma college sports (Zonal Sports) and inter zonal sport events

Activities / Events Under Gymkhana

Sr. No. Activity / Event Dates Activity
01 Every year after completion of all admission process Formation of Students Gymkhana Committee
02 In the month of Jan–Feb every year Interclass and IEDSSA sports
03 Throughout the academic year Debate competition, poster competition, technical exhibition, and social events such as new voter registration and awareness campaign
04 Once in academic year Annual Cultural Gathering
