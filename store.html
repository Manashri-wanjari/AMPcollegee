About Co-operative Store

The Co-operative Store in our premises plays an important role in supporting the needs of students by providing essential academic and daily use items at affordable prices. The store operates on a co-operative basis, ensuring that goods are supplied at reasonable rates without the intention of heavy profit-making. Students can easily access textbooks, stationery, drawing instruments, lab materials, and other required items within the campus itself, saving both time and effort.

Facilities Available in Co-operative Store

  • Textbooks for all courses
  • Stationery items like pens, pencils, notebooks, and drawing instruments
  • Lab materials and consumables for practicals
  • Daily essentials like toiletries, snacks, and beverages
  • Affordable pricing on all items due to co-operative model
